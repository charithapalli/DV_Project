Gross National Product is the total income earned by a country's residents and businesses, including any income from abroad, divided by the population of the country.

It reflects the average income of a country's citizens, converted to US dollars using the World Bank method. The choropleth map shows Gross National Product across different countries with respective to the Year. Different shades of color indicate the Gross National Product range, with darker colors representing higher Gross National Product. The user can input a year to view the economic status of countries for that particular year.
